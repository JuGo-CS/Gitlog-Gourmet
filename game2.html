How to Play — Code Breaker

1 A secret 4-item food combo is hidden (Fruit, Main, Drink, Dessert).
2 Pick one food from each category in the cabinet below. 🍲
3 Click Submit to check your guess against the secret combo.
4 🔴 Rule: Each row (category) has only 1 correct item, and each column (item value) appears only once across all categories!
